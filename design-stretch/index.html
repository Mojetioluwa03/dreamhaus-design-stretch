FACILITATOR

MichaEL Light T.

MichaEL Light Tommy is a creative leader working at the intersection of human experience, visual creativity, and technology. With over a decade of experience, he has led high-impact design, research, and creative projects for leading organizations and brands including Honeywell Group, Co-creation Hub (CcHub), Sympli, Ennovate Lab, and KTN–UK Aid.

He currently serves as a User Experience Researcher at YouVersion's Bible App, while also leading DreamHaus as Founder and Creative Director, and co-leading L'Orbe Creative Excellence Initiative, a nonprofit advancing creative excellence and human flourishing across Africa's secondary cities.

MichaEL is widely recognized for designing Minister Dunsin Oyekan's album covers between 2022 and 2024, as well as the cover art for Folabi Nuel's Glory On The Other Side featuring Grammy Award winner Matt Redman. Through Marvelly's God Is My Designer project, his work has reached audiences in more than five countries across Africa and North America.

His work has been featured on CNN Africa, YNaija, and Afrocritik, and he has facilitated training and spoken at platforms including the National Information Technology Development Agency (NITDA), TEDx, the Ogbomoso Digital Economy Summit (ODES), and Google Developer Student Groups (GDSC/GDSG).

Driven by a passion for God, family, creativity, and community, MichaEL is committed to raising a new generation of purpose-driven creatives who design with excellence and contribute meaningfully to society. He lives in Ogbomoso, Nigeria, with his wife, Otobong, and their three children.

Design Stretch is designed primarily for junior and intermediate designers across different design disciplines.

The experience is open to graphic designers, brand designers, UI/UX designers, product designers, and other creatives.

Design Stretch combines guided learning, practical exercises, design challenges, and critique.
